🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

FillerSkip ("we", "us", "our") is a free anime filler episode tracking tool. Our website is accessible at fillerskip.netlify.app and any future domains we may operate.

What Data We Collect

FillerSkip does not collect any personal information. All data you create — your watchlist, episode progress, and preferences — is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and never sent to any server. We do not have a database of users.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit our site and which anime guides are most popular. This data is anonymous and aggregated. We do not use tracking cookies to identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Affiliate Links

FillerSkip contains affiliate links to streaming platforms including Crunchyroll, Funimation, Netflix, and Amazon Prime Video. If you click these links and make a purchase or sign up for a subscription, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These commissions help us keep the site free and add more anime guides.

Advertising

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense or similar ad networks. These networks may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing behaviour. You can opt out of personalised advertising at your Google account settings or via the NAI opt-out tool at optout.networkadvertising.org.

Third Party Services

Our site uses Google Fonts for typography which may log your IP address when loading font files. We use Netlify for hosting which may collect basic server access logs including IP addresses. These are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Children

FillerSkip is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we store no personal data on our servers, there is nothing to delete or export on our end. To clear your local data (watchlist and progress), you can clear your browser's localStorage at any time via your browser settings.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our anime suggestion form on the website. We will respond as soon as possible.

📋 How to Deploy on Netlify (5 Minutes)
1
Go to netlify.com and create a free account (use your Google or GitHub login)
2
On the dashboard, find the section that says "Deploy manually" or drag and drop to deploy
3
Put your fillerskip-full.html file inside a folder, rename the HTML file to index.html first
4
Drag that folder into the Netlify deploy area — it gives you a free URL instantly like amazing-site-123.netlify.app
5
Go to Site Settings → Change site name and rename it to fillerskip.netlify.app — free custom subdomain
6
Share your link everywhere — Reddit, Discord, anime communities. You're live! 🎉
